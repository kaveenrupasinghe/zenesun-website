About

Who we are

ZENESUN CO, LTD is a dynamic company established in February 2016, located in Fuji City, Shizuoka Prefecture, Japan. With a capital of 5 million yen, we specialize in the design, manufacturing, processing, sales, and import/export of a wide range of molds and plastic products. Our expertise extends to various metal materials, jigs and tools, as well as automatic machines.

Our President and CEO, Shu You, leads our dedicated team with a vision to innovate and excel in the manufacturing sector.

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction drives us to continuously improve our processes and expand our offerings in the competitive market.

At ZENESUN CO., LTD, we pride ourselves on delivering high-quality products and services that meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our core business activities include:

  • Molds: Comprehensive design and manufacturing services for various molds.
  • Plastic Products: Production and sales of an array of plastic items tailored to client specifications.
  • Metal Materials and Tools: Design and manufacturing of metal materials, jigs, tools, and automatic machines for various industrial applications.
